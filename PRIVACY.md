\# Scanables Privacy Policy



Scanables does not collect, sell, or share personal data.



Saved barcodes and QR codes are stored locally on your device.



Camera access is used only to scan barcodes and QR codes.



Export and backup files are saved locally to your device. They are only shared if you choose to share them using Android’s share options.



Scanables does not require an account and does not upload saved scanables to a server.
